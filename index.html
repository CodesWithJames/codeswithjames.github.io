DuckyHost

A easy and secure way to host your personal images.

What do we do?

We store your images on a secure domain with very good loading speeds and when you want them we will open the for you and your friends!

Usage

The DuckyHost can be used for a variety of purposes, but it can be mainly used as a permanent way to store images for work, school or just a way to upload your images to not have to save them! With DuckyHost every image gets a url

Privacy

Your images are private unless someone has the link. The hoster is anonymous and we do not track uploader data. Therefore our speeds and images have better quality.

File Names

File names are randomly generated. They contain a mix of alphanumerical letters including '-'. This is to keep your links as safe as possible.

Restrictions

Please do not post inappropriate images or copyrighted images. I will not be responsible for any uploaded images. Your image might be terminated if you are caught posting illegal acts or innapropriate images.

Storage Time

Images in theory will be stored forever, however the database may be cleared from time to time for maintainence purposes.
